How it works

Rule-based, not a black box

Every decision is explainable. No machine learning guesswork — just transparent regex rules you can read on GitHub.

01

You paste or upload

03

Score = 50 + (green weight) − (red weight), clamped 0–100. Every triggered rule shows you exactly why.

Detection signals

What the engine looks for

Red Flags — raise the risk score
  • 40 Upfront payment demand (deposit, training fee, courier)
  • 25 Offer sent from Gmail / Yahoo / personal email
  • 20 Unrealistic salary structure (per-day/hour pay)
  • 20 Requests sensitive documents before onboarding
  • 15 No physical company address found
  • 15 CIN / GST registration number missing
  • 15 Work-from-home + suspiciously high salary combo
  • 10 Urgency language ("reply in 24 hours", "last chance")
  • 10 No HR name or designation in the letter
  • 10 Multiple spelling / grammar errors
Green Flags — lower the risk score
  • 20 Email from a verified corporate domain
  • 20 CIN number present (verifiable on MCA portal)
  • 10 Specific joining date mentioned
  • 10 Named Indian city / office location
  • 10 Probation period or notice period stated
  • 10 Job designation clearly labelled
  • 10 PF, ESI, gratuity, or CTC breakdown present
  • 10 Signed by a named authority or director
70–100 Likely Genuine
40–69 Suspicious
0–39 High Risk
