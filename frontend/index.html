Detection signals

What the engine looks for

Red Flags — raise the risk score
  • 40pts Upfront payment demand (deposit, training fee, courier)
  • 25pts Offer sent from Gmail / Yahoo / personal email
  • 20pts Unrealistic salary structure (per-day/hour pay)
  • 20pts Requests sensitive documents before onboarding
  • 15pts No physical company address found
  • 15pts CIN / GST registration number missing
  • 15pts Work-from-home + suspiciously high salary combo
  • 10pts Urgency language ("reply in 24 hours", "last chance")
  • 10pts No HR name or designation in the letter
  • 10pts Multiple spelling / grammar errors
Green Flags — lower the risk score
  • 20pts Email from a verified corporate domain
  • 20pts CIN number present (verifiable on MCA portal)
  • 10pts Specific joining date mentioned
  • 10pts Named Indian city / office location
  • 10pts Probation period or notice period stated
  • 10pts Job designation clearly labelled
  • 10pts PF, ESI, gratuity, or CTC breakdown present
  • 10pts Signed by a named authority or director
70 – 100 Likely Genuine
40 – 69 Suspicious
0 – 39 High Risk — Possible Scam
